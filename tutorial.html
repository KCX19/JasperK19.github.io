Exp Lane

The Exp Lane is a lane for when you eliminate the EXP-carrying minions, it will make you level up quickly.(Fighter)

Gold Lane

Gold Laners are pretty weak in the early game, but they can earn more gold to buy equipment faster(Marksman)

Jungler

The Jungler also known as the "Core" usually relies on farming the jungle to gain XP and gold, which make the player stronger.(Assassin)

Mid Lane

The Mid Lane is the only 1vs1 lane.Usually relies on mechanical skill on your part.(Mage)

Roaming

The Roam goes to another lane to help killing enemies, or defending the turret.(Tank/Support)

Roles

  • ★ Marksman is a type of role that has a high attack/damage and critical attacks froma far range.Marksman plays an important role in the game by defeating enemies from a distance.
  • ★ Tank is a type of role that has high HP and high defense while having a very low attack. This position is always at the front by acting as a shield against enemies, taking the damage.
  • ★ Support heroes protect their allies in battles. Their main role is to aid their teammates. Support heroes help mobile legends heroes that are less durable and easy to kill, such as the marksman heroes.
  • ★ Fighter is a role that carries a mix of capabilities in battle, including defensive and offensive stats. Even though Fighters aren't known for being the starters in team fights or the one with most damage, their skills may soon turn into a major threat.
  • ★ Mage cause damage to nearby enemies using magic. Given this, mage heroes are highly important in any team. Their main strength is not their physical attack but their ability effects.
  • ★ Assassin is very agile that specializes in killing or disabling roaming targets picking them of as they try to replenish HP. Assassins choose their targets and aim at them carefully.

Information of Skills

  • ✤ CC (crowd control) is an ability that can immobilize the enemy. Like Stun, Airborne, Silent or restrictive opponents like the Cyclops. CC skills usually belong to tanks.
  • ✤ Blink is a skill that enables us to move quickly. Mostly used to pursue goals, not infrequently also to escape. Almost all assassins and fighters have this type of skill.
  • ✤ AoE: Area of Effect, is a skill that can damage more than 1 target. Can be straight, wide, or round / circular.
  • ✤ Buff: This is a skill that can strengthen our hero's attributes, such as speeding up movement, speeding up attack speed, increasing armor and physical attacks, etc.

Tips and Tricks

  1. ❖ Before using a hero in an actual match, gamers are advised to first read the hero's skills. If reading is too much trouble, players can also utilize the Training Ground to try the hero's out. To access it, open the Training Camp and choose Practice.
  2. ❖ Throughout each match, players can check everyone's equipment. This may seem overwhelming for beginners, but it's a crucial habit to have. In Mobile Legends, there are three types of damage; Magic, Physical, and True Damage. Each has a counter of its own.
  3. ❖ Hitting Turret Grants Gold. In the first five minutes of the game, if players are handling a Lane, it's important to remember that the protected Turret will grant Gold if it's damaged.
  4. ❖ Do not attack a turret if there is no minions in range of the turret. You will take huge amount of damage attacking the turret without the minion in range.
  5. ❖ Constantly look at the map. If we keep an eye on the minimap, we can avoid being ganked or alert our team of a potential enemy ganks.
  6. ❖ Turrets before kills.
  7. ❖ When you want to gank, you always have to communicate and make sure it's at least 4 of you, so you have a higher chance of winning the gank without deaths, and everyone can go back to their lanes.
