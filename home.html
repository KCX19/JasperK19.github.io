Welcome to Mobile Legend

Mobile Legends: Bang Bang is a 5vs5 multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game designed for mobile phones.

Goal:The main objective of the game is to destroy the enemy base, most importantly the nexus, while killing other enemy heroes and destroying turrets in the process. You will be assisted by spawned AI units or minions who fight forward with you along the set paths.

There are currently 122 heroes in Mobile Legend

6 roles for different heroes

  • Fighter
  • Marksman
  • Assassin
  • Mage
  • Tank
  • Support
